AGRC's is located at the Utah State Capitol Complex in the State Office Building -- approximately 450 N East Capitol Blvd (150 E.) in Salt Lake City.

Office Hours are Monday - Friday 8 am - 5 pm.

AGRC offices are on the 5th floor, room 5130.
