AGRC's is located at the Utah State Capitol Complex in the State Office Building -- approximately 450 N East Capitol Blvd (150 E.) in Salt Lake City.

Office Hours are Monday - Friday 8 am - 5 pm.

AGRC offices are on the 5th floor, room 5130.

The Utah State Capitol is located in the northern area of Salt Lake City, UT. It is easily accessible to the Salt Lake City International Airport and the University of Utah.Â  It is a 5 to 10 minute drive to the I-15 freeway (600 North exit), which connects us to the rest of the state.

UTA runs a free bus, #500, that runs from downtown to the Capitol.
