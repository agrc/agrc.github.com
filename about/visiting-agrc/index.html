The Utah State Capitol is located in the northern area of Salt Lake City, UT. It is easily accessible to the Salt Lake City International Airport and the University of Utah.Â  It is a 5 to 10 minute drive to the I-15 freeway (600 North exit), which connects us to the rest of the state. Getting Here

AGRC is located in the Utah State Capitol Complex inside the State Office Building. The approximate address is 450 N East Capitol Blvd (150 E.) in Salt Lake City. The AGRC office is on the 5th floor in room 5130. The public entrance is north of the fountain behind the State Capitol building. Upon entering the building, there will be elevators and stairs on the right past the receptionist. Make your way up to the fifth floor, wrap around to the left and AGRC's doorbell will be on the last door on the left past the bathrooms.
