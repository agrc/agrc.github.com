Central Utah User Group (CUUG) members are made up of state, local, and federal agencies, and private organizations throughout Juab, Millard, Piute, Sanpete, Sevier, and Wayne Counties. CUUG focuses on professional development in GIS, discussing technical issues, and sharing GIS information. New members are always welcome! For more information, please contact the Chair, Jason Anderson: jranders@blm.gov

CUUG meets quarterly on the 4th Thursday of January, March, June, and September in Richfield, Utah.

The next CUUG meeting will be held on January 23rd, 2014 at the BLM Richfield Field Office.
