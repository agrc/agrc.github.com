Historic Lake Bonneville

This data set represents an approximation of the Bonneville level of historic Lake Bonneville and does not take into consideration isostatic rebound. This dataset was created by locating the historic shoreline on NAIP imagery and USGS quadrangles and generating a contour at that location using USGS 10 meter DEM for the base elevation.
