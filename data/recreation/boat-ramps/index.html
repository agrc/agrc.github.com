Boat Ramps

Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): AGRC and DNR

Abstract:
This dataset contains Utah Boat Ramps including State Parks, some National Park Service and other ramps.
This dataset also contains the name of the boat ramp, the water body it is on, the type of vessels that can be launched, and the owner, agency, and administrator of the ramp.

Usage: There are no constraints or warranties with regard to the use of this dataset.
