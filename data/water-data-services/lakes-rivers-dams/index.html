NHD Lakes, Rivers, Streams, Springs


Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): AGRC & USGS

Abstract:
The Lakes, Rivers, Streams & Springs data package contains feature layers that are derived from the National Hydrography Dataset (NHD). The NHD is the surface water component of the National Map created by the United States Geological Survey (USGS). The NHD contains a feature-based database that interconnects and uniquely identifies the stream segments or reaches that make up the nation's surface water drainage system. NHD data was originally developed at 1:100,000-scale and exists at that scale for the whole country. NHD data in it's entirety can be downloaded from here (ex. NHDH_UT_).

Data Content:
The following data is available for download:

NHD Lakes

Water.LakesNHDHighRes was derived from the NHD. The derivation and modification process has been performed by AGRC to provide a general purpose feature class of water bodies. Two fields have been added to this feature class (InUtah and IsMajor) and features have been split at the state boundary. General Utah GIS data inquiries: agrc@utah.gov
