DEQ Water-Related Monitoring

  • Data Type: GIS Data Layer
  • Steward(s): Utah Department of Environmental Quality
  • Web Application: DEQ Interactive Map Viewer
  • Abstract: The DEQ's Division of Water Quality is responsible for safe drinking water and monitoring water quality. Below are DEQ datasets related to protection zones for drinking water. To view all DEQ geographic data online, see the DEQ Interactive Map Viewer

Package Contents

Monitored Lakes

Environment.DWQMonitoredLakes132 contains locations of the 132 lakes in Utah monitored for water quality by the Division of Water Quality (Department of Environmental Quality). Environment.LakeMonitoring contains 729 Monitored Lakes. Each location is a point, which is located within the lake's high water level shoreline.
