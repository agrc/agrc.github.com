DAQ Air Emissions Inventory

Download Dataset

DEQ Air-Related Contaminant and Cleanup Sites

Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): Utah Department of Environmental Quality

Abstract:
Environment.DAQAirEmissionsInventory depicts facility locations and annual emissions. Annual emissions (in tons) are included for ammonia, pm10, carbon monoxide, sulfer dioxide, volatile organic compounds, and oxides of nitogen for Utah. To view all DEQ geographic data online, see the DEQ Interactive Map Viewer.
