DAQ Air-Related Contaminant and Cleanup Sites

  • Data Type: GIS Data Layer
  • Steward(s): Utah Department of Environmental Quality
  • Web Application: DEQ Interactive Map Viewer
  • Abstract: Environment.DAQAirEmissionsInventory depicts facility locations and annual emissions.
