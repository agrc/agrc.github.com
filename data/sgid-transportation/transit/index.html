Download Data Package

Bus, Commuter, and Light Rail


Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): Utah Transit Authority (UTA)

Abstract:
This dataset contains the UTA bus route and stop locations, commuter rail route and stop locations, and light rail route, and stop locations. The extent of these data is primarily along the Wasatch Front.

Data Content:
The following data is available for download:

UTA Bus Routes & Stops

[tabs]
[tab title="Description"]Transportation.BusRoutes_UTA includes bus stops along streets, at TRAX stations, and park & ride lots serviced by UTA buses. Transportation.BusStops_UTA (as of August 2015) includes redesigned bus routes that have been adjusted for internal monitoring purposes and ski bus routes to the Cottonwood Canyons and Sundance Ski Resort.

[/tab]

[tab title="Usage"]
The UTA Bus Stops dataset was digitized to the Wasatch Front counties' street base maps and then exported from Trapeze (scheduling software) in shapefile format.

The native spatial reference for this dataset is UTM Zone 12N, NAD83 (0.01 coordinate precision). [/tab]

[tab title="Updates"] Updates to this dataset are published periodically.

Last Update: December 2016

[/tab]

UTA Light Rail

[tabs]
[tab title="Description"]Transportation.LightRail_UTA represents the light rail system for the main North-South (Downtown to Sandy), University/Medical Center, West Valley, Daybreak, and Airport lines, as of May 2013. It also depicts the Sugar House trolley extension currently under construction.

[/tab]

[tab title="Usage"]
LightRail_UTA was digitized from aerial photographs dated July, 2012.

The native spatial reference for this dataset is UTM Zone 12N, NAD83 (0.01 coordinate precision).
[/tab]

[tab title="Updates"] Updates to this dataset are published periodically.
