Oil and Gas Wells

This dataset depicts oil and gas well points in Utah from the Utah Department of Natural Resources, Oil Gas and Mining Division. The dataset contains the API code, well and company name, account number, filed number, field name, elevation, locations coordinates, lease numbers, well type and status, total cumulative oil, gas and water, and more. The path (HDPath) and bottom of hole (HDBottom) for horizontally-drilled wells are available as a separate feature classes derived from attributes in the wells feature class (see Links|Download tab).

Field Descriptions:

DIRECTION: Flag showing if well is dirctionally drilled (D) or horizontally drilled (H)
WELL_STATUS_MAIN: The most current well status available. See below for coded values.
WELL_TYPE_MAIN: The most current well type available. See below for coded values.
SURF_OWNER_TYPE: Surface owner flag (1-Federal, 2-Indian, 3-State, 4-Fee)
GIS_STAT_TYPE: Combination of Well_type_main and Well_status_main to be used on maps

Information on the coded values used in the 'WELL_STATUS_MAIN' and 'WELL_TYPE_MAIN' fields.

Other Field Descriptions:

ACCT_NUM: DOGM Operator account number
FIELD_NUM: DOGM Oil/Gas field number
LOCATION_SURF_WCR: Location surface footages from Well Completion Report
CONF_FLAG: Confidential status flag
CONF_DATE: Confidential status date
LEASE_TYPE: Mineral lease type (1-Federal, 2-Indian, 3-State, 4-Fee)
LA_PA_DATE: Termination date of an APD or well - Location Abandoned (permit rescinded) or Plugged and Abandoned (well plugged)
MODIFY_DATE: The date the DOGM database record was last changed
TOTAL_CUM_OIL: Total cumulative production volume of crude oil for the lifetime of the well in BBLS (barrels - one barrel equals 42 U.S. gallons)
TOTAL_CUM_GAS: Total cumulative production volume of natural gas for the lifetime of the well in MCF (1 MCF equals one thousand cubic feet)
TOTAL_CUM_WATER: Total cumulative production volume of water for the lifetime of the well in BBLS (barrels - one barrel equals 42 U.S. gallons)
MULTI_LEG_COUNT: A count from well completion reports showing how many 'laterals' or 'legs' a horizontal well has
CBMETHANE_FLAG: Flag identifies wells that are classified as coalbed methane gas wells
BOND_NUMBER: Plugging bond number
BOND_TYPE: Plugging bond type (1-Federal, 2-Indian, 3-State, 4-Fee)
CA_NUMBER: Communitization Agreement number
FIELD_TYPE_FLAG: Flag identifies the field type at the time the well is permitted (D-development, E-extension, W-wildcat)
ORIG_COMPL_DATE: Date from the Well Completion Report of when drilling and testing was completed
ORIG_TD: Total Depth of drilling - Measured Depth - from WCR
API: The API number (American Petroleum Institute standard numbering system) assigned to the well by the State of Utah
WELL_NAME: The name and number of a well as assigned by the operating company
FIELD_NAME: The name of the oil or gas field in which the well is located
ELEVATION: The well elevation in feet above sea level (GR=Ground Reading, DF=Derrick Floor, KB=Kelly Busing)
COORDS_SURF_N: Universal Transverse Mercator (UTM) surface coordinates - Northings (y coordinate); UTM Zone 12, NAD83
COORDS_SURF_E: Universal Transverse Mercator (UTM) surface coordinates - Eastings (x coordinate); UTM Zone 12, NAD83
COORDS_BHL_N: Universal Transverse Mercator (UTM) bottom hole coordinates - Northings (y coordinate); UTM Zone 12, NAD83
COORDS_BHL_E: Universal Transverse Mercator (UTM) bottom hole coordinates - Eastings (x coordinate); UTM Zone 12, NAD83
QTR_QTR: Well location - quarter/quarter section
SECTION: Well location - section
TOWNSHIP: Well location - township
RANGE: Well location - range
MERIDIAN: Well location - meridian (Salt Lake or Uintah meridians)
COUNTY: Well location - county
ALT_ADDRESS: Alternate address flag; works mainly with ACCT_NUM field for mail routing purposes
LEASE_NUM: Current mineral lease number assigned to a well as reported by well operator
INDIAN_TRIBE: Designates Native American Indian allottee or tribe if applicable ('Navajo' or 'Ute'
UNIT_NAME: The name of the unit if applicable. A unit is a joint operation of separately owned leases
LAT_SURF: Well location latitude coordinates in degrees; UTM Zone 12, NAD83
LONG_SURF: Well location latitude coordinates in degrees; UTM Zone 12, NAD83
COMPANY_NAME: The name of the well operating company
