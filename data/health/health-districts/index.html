Health Districts 2015

Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): Utah Department of Health & AGRC

Abstract:
Health.HealthDistricts2015 contains the health districts in the state of Utah. These districts are often the main entity for reporting and tracking communicable diseases, as well as providing services to the community and state that include investigation of disease outbreaks, health education, health screenings and immunizations, and food regulation for establishments.
