Enterprise Zones

Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): AGRC and GOED

Abstract:
Economy.EnterpriseZones are areas created by the Utah Governors Office of Economic Development to encourage economic growth and development in specified areas by providing tax breaks to development projects within these boundaries. Economy.EnterpriseZones was originally created to be used in a web application that allows the public to search by address and be informed whether or not a property is located within an Enterprise Zone.
