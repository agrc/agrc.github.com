Liquor Stores

Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): AGRC & DABC

Abstract:
Society.LiquorStores contains information and locations for liquor stores and package agencies controlled by the Department of Alcoholic Beverage Control of Utah. The source of this dataset is the Utah Department of Alcoholic Beverage Control's online map.
