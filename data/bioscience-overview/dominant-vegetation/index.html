Dominant Vegetation

[tab title="Description"]This dataset represents the statewide distribution of dominant vegetation species in Utah. The data were derived from 1:24,000 scale orthophoto quadrangles, 1:40,000 scale aerial photos, and field mapping by the Utah Division of Wildlife Resources.
