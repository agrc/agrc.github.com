These small area projections are developed from county-level totals projected by the University of Utah Gardner Policy Institute. Two small area geographies are available: Traffic Analysis Zone (TAZ's) polygons and City Area polygons. TAZ's average about 1 square mile in size along the Wasatch Front but are larger in rural areas. City Areas are approximations of city boundaries built by combining TAZ polygons. All links point at ‘open data’ pages from which shapefiles, KML, CSV, and web services can be downloaded or utilized." %}
