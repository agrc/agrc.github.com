Aquifers

Download Data Package

Aquifers


Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): AGRC, USGS and DEQ

Abstract:
The Aquifers data package contains the Recharge Discharge Areas and the Basin Fill Boundaries along the Wasatch Front and adjacent areas.

Data Content:
The following data is available for download:

Recharge Discharge Areas

Geoscience.Aquifer_RechargeDischargeAreas contains the recharge and discharge areas of the principal aquifers along the Wasatch Front and adjacent areas.

AGRC or M.E. Jensen, Utah Department of Environmental Quality, Division of Drinking Water

Basin Fill Boundary

Geoscience.Aquifer_BasinFillBoundary contains the approximate boundaries of basin fill in ground-water recharge and discharge areas of the principal aquifers along the Wasatch Front and adjacent areas.
