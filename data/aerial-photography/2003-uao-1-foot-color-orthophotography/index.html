2003 UAO 1 Foot Orthophotography

AGRC has Urban Area Ortho-Imagery (UAO) 1-foot resolution color aerial photography from 2003 for the Wasatch Front north of Utah County.

  • Download - 2003 UAO Interactive Map
  • Retrieve UAO imagery via FTP site
  • Use our Image Server

UAO_Index is a shapefile that can be used to determine what images to download. The images have a UTM NAD83 meters projection and are from 2003. Image flight dates can be found in the .xml file of the associated image tile.

The naming convention for the imagery tiles is based off the U.S. National Grid (USNG). Utah is in USNG grid zone 12T and 12S.
