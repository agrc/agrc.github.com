AGRC has statewide National Agricultural Imagery Program (NAIP) natural color aerial photography for Utah. The imagery is 1-meter resolution and was collected in the summer of 2009. The date the photography was flown can be found in the SrcImgDate field of the NAIP2009_Index  shapefile for the individual TIFF files or the SDATE field of the county mosaics index file. The images have a UTM NAD83 meters projection.
