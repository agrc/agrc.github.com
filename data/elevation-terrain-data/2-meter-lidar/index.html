LiDAR2006_2m_BareEarth_Tiles is a shapefile that can be used to determine what files to download in the Wasatch Front area. The Heber Valley and Iron County areas download as complete areas in a .zip file. These LiDAR files are in a .asc format and are around 10 mb per 2,000 X 2,000 meter block. This data has a UTM NAD83 NAVD88 Meters zone 12 Geoid 03 projection. If you have any trouble contact Rick Kelson  at (801-538-3237)
