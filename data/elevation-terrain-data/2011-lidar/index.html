AGRC has 1 meter LiDAR in .img and .las format for various areas of the state collected during 2011. The DEMs are available from our FTP site but the DSM and .85 meter classified point clouds are available by request or from Open Topography. The areas include the north, east and south sides of the Great Salt Lake, Cedar Valley, Hurricane Fault and more.

Download a geodatabase or a group of shapefiles of all the tile indices and boundaries that can be used to determine what imagery files to download. The LiDAR DEM files are in a .img format and are around 10 mb per 2,000 X 2,000 meter block. This data has a UTM NAD83 NAVD88 Meters zone 12 Geoid 08 projection.
