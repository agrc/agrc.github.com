500 Foot Contours


Data Type: GIS Data Layer
Steward(s): AGRC
Abstract:
This datasets contain a statewide coverage of 500 foot contours derived from the 10 meter National Elevation Dataset's (NED) Digital Elevation Model (DEM).


Data Content:
The following data is available for download:

500 Foot Contours

[tabs]
[tab title="Description"]ELEVATION.Contours500Ft is a statewide dataset of contour lines at a 500 foot interval for cartography and approximate elevation identification.

[/tab]

[tab title="Usage"]Elevation information is stored in the 500 Foot contour lines in an attribute ELEV associated with each contour isoline.

The native spatial reference for this dataset is UTM Zone 12N, NAD83.

There are no constraints or warranties with regard to the use of this dataset. Users are encouraged to attribute content to: State of Utah, SGID.
[/tab]

[tab title="Updates"]This dataset has not been updated recently.
[/tab]
