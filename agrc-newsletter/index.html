AGRC distributes a monthly newsletter that features AGRC news and other statewide GIS announcements. If you have an item you would like included in the newsletter, contact Jessie at jpechmann@utah.gov. Map spotlights, events, and other technical news from the community are much appreciated. Subscribe to the newsletter at the bottom of any webpage on gis.utah.gov.
