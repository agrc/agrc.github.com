AGRC distributes a monthly newsletter that features AGRC news and other statewide GIS announcements. If you have an item you would like included in the newsletter, contact Jessie at jpechmann@utah.gov. Map spotlights, events, and other technical news from the community are always welcome. Subscribe to the newsletter at the bottom of any webpage on gis.utah.gov. All individual blogposts are available on the news page.
