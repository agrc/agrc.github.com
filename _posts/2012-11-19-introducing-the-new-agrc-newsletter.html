On November 14, AGRC sent the first edition of a short-format newsletter to subscribers who signed up on the gis.utah.gov website.

The newsletter features recent announcements, blog posts and other information relevant to Utah's GIS and mapping community.

To receive our newsletter, fill out the 'Newsletter' form found in the footer section of any pages on gis.utah.gov.

The UGIC mailing list is separate from the AGRC mailing list, signup for the UGIC mailing on their website by becoming a UGIC member.
