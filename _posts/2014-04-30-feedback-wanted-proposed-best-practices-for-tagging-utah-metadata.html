AGRC has developed a draft 'best practices' document for assigning metadata tags to esri 'Item Description'-style metadata.

Tags play an important role in search optimization in both ArcGIS Online and Esri's newly released Open Data platform.

The creation of this draft was driven by AGRC preparations for the GIS data resources in Utah's State Geographic Information Database (SGID) to work optimally these two and other environments.

Feedback on the best practices document is encouraged and can be provided within the google doc by right clicking and selecting 'Comment'. Thank you in advance for your input!
