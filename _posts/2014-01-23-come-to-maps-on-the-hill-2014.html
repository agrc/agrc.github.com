Maps on the Hill is an annual event sponsored by UGIC, and held during the Utah legislative session as a means for professional and student mapmakers in Utah to share their maps with elected officials and the public.

When: Wednesday, January 29, 2014: Displays will be up from 10:30am-12:30pm
Where: Utah State Capitol Rotunda
What: Maps! Topics include public safety, natural resources, infrastructure & utilities, regional planning, emergency management, recreation, and transportation.

Get a sneak peak of the event in the Maps on the Hill Map Book 2014. This is a great opportunity to learn about some of the cool maps people are working on in Utah, and to meet the mapmakers.

Please share this flyer with anybody who might be interested. Parking will be limited at the Capitol, carpooling or public transit are suggested.
